Welcome to The Ambulance Navigation about us section

Ambulance Navugation System is a web site created for emergency situations.The website focuses on two things.

1.On the Patients Side :

The Patient will be able to call a nearby ambulance for aid and aslo show the Patient the time remaining in the arrival of ambulance.The Patient will also be able to track the ambulance on its way

2.On the Ambulance Driver's Side:

The call will be requested with details of the destination displayed on the map the website along with the shortest path to the destination

The project makes use of mapmyindia api for the purpose of all map related operations.
