Select the "Call and Ambulance Option" and you will be redirected onto the next page. There, you can call an ambulance on one tap and track its progress on the map make sure you are not connected to a vpn so that your accurate location can be provided to the ambulance driver.

The route with the least traffic zones and the location of the patients will be displayed on the screen.
